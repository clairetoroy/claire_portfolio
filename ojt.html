OJT Experience

Practical training experience at Mega Fishing Corporation

Mega Fishing Corporation

On-the-Job Training Program

Technical Skills & Knowledge Acquired

I learned various skills such as proper document formatting for the letters my supervisor needed. I also picked up new formulas that made it easier for me to encode data. I gained proper knowledge on using the company's internal system called SAP. My job was to get familiar with and use the system. I learned how to input, create, extract, and delete information using different features. These skills helped me adjust quickly to new technologies and use them effectively for my work.

Workplace Collaboration & Interactions

During my OJT experience, I had regular interactions with my colleagues, importantly my supervisor, which significantly contributed to my everyday growth. I worked closely with my supervisor who guided me through daily tasks, provided clear instructions, and offered feedback to help me improve. Through other colleagues also, I learned how to communicate effectively in a professional setting, ask appropriate questions if they gave me tasks, and meet deadlines. I also observed how my colleagues handle different responsibilities given, and maintain professionalism in the workplace. From these interactions, I learned the importance of respect, and adaptability. Overall, collaboration helped me develop a proper work ethic and confidence in a real-work environment.

Challenges & How I Overcame Them

One of the main obstacles I encountered was effective communication in a workplace setting. I found it challenging to ask questions confidently, because I was shy and introverted. To overcome this challenge, I made a conscious effort to improve my communication skills by actively listening and taking notes on how the other employees actually talk professionally to each other. Over time, my efforts improved my confidence to talk to my colleagues, and perform my responsibilities more efficiently.

Most Notable Experience

One of the most notable experiences I had during my OJT was the 15 minute break. It taught me as a person that it is okay to rest. It is okay to pause things out. That 15 minute break feels like a breath of fresh air, that even in a professional setting, people have to rest their mind at some point to avoid exhausting themselves.

Alignment with Academic Goals

It aligned with my academic goals and program objectives by allowing me to apply concepts I learned in school to real workplace situations. The tasks assigned to me helped in improving my technical skills, and problem-solving. This alignment helped in narrowing the gap between classroom learning and actual industry practices, making the lessons I learned academically more relevant and meaningful. The OJT experience encouraged me to become more responsible, disciplined, and adaptable, which supported my academic goal of becoming a competent and job-ready professional.

OJT Program Evaluation

Strengths

  • Provided hands-on experience that helped me in an everyday work setting
  • Supportive supervisor and colleagues that offered proper guidance
  • Actual practices in the industry that may help in future professional settings

Weaknesses

  • No training sessions
  • Not many chances to do more than the assigned tasks

Recommendations

  • Include training sessions to enhance different skills
  • Let the interns work in different departments of the company

Company Support & Guidance

They gave me a proper time to learn things through proper guidance and instructions. Through different seminars and events, I enhanced my skills on how to communicate professionally to other people. My colleagues from the company are easy to be with, with the right boundaries and still professional. Different task gave me new knowledge and learning that I will be applying in the future.

Exposure to Professional Aspects

My OJT experience exposed me to the practical and professional aspects of my field by allowing me to apply my knowledge on some tasks. I met deadlines properly and strictly. I am hands-on on every task I was given. Being able to be responsible for every work.

Key Takeaways & Advice

Advice for Future OJT Students

Be adaptive and open to new learnings and knowledge. Know how to receive proper feedback and guidance, and also ask things for clarifications. Communicate professionally to your colleagues for they can also help you if you have a question or having a hard time doing the given tasks.

New Perspectives Gained

During my OJT, I gained a new perspective about the importance of collaboration. In order to survive in a professional setting, you really have to collaborate with your colleagues, for your colleagues can be the people who will help you to get by everyday no matter how hard the work is.
